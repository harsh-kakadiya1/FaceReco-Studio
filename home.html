How Face Recognition Works

Upload Reference

Start by uploading a clear reference image containing the face you want to find. Our AI analyzes facial features and creates a unique biometric template.

AI Analysis

Advanced neural networks detect faces, extract key features like eyes, nose, mouth, and facial structure to create a comprehensive face signature.

Batch Processing

Upload multiple images or entire collections. Our system processes them efficiently in batches, comparing each face against your reference.

Smart Matching

Get precise confidence scores for each match. Adjust tolerance levels to find exact matches or similar faces based on your needs.

Results Analysis

Review detailed results with confidence scores, similarity metrics, and visual comparisons to validate matches.

Privacy Protection

Your data is processed locally and securely. No images are stored on our servers, ensuring complete privacy.
